Pakistani Falooda

Perfect Pakistani Hot Summer Dessert!

Just 15 steps!

Total time: 30 minutes

Servings: 5 people

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Chopped Almonds
  • Chopped Pistachios
  • 2 tbsp chia seeds or basil seeds
  • 1 pack falooda noodles or rice noodlesüçú
  • 1 original malai kulfi (found in desi stores)üç®
  • Rooh Afza (found in desi stores)
  • 1 packet strawberry jellyüçì

Instructions

  1. Prepare jelly by using a packet of powdered jelly. You can use any flavour you like.

  2. Then slice up the prepared jelly into squares.

  3. Traditionally, basil seeds are added but chia seeds could also be used instead. Put 2 tbsp of seeds into a bowl.

  4. Add plenty of water and leave the seeds to soak for 20-30 minutes until they become soft.

  5. Prepare one pack of falooda noodles, which could be bought from Asian stores. Rice noodles work as well.

  6. After cooling the noodles down, cut them into small noodles.

  7. In order to prepare the Rooh Afza milk. Add some milk in a pan and cook it for few minutes until thick.

  8. After cooling down the milk add some Rooh Afza (Pakistani sweet rose syrup) and stir it.

  9. Add some noodles in each serving glass (milkshake glasses recommanded).

  10. Threw in some strawberry jelly and seeds.

  11. Add more than 1 scope of frozen original malai kulfi or ice cream.

  12. Add another layer of ingredients if needed.

  13. Pour some of the Roh Afza milk into the falooda.

  14. Top it of with some chopped Almonds and Pishtachios to sprinkle.

  15. Serve it with a spoon and a straw. Enjoy! üçπ
